**Adcock Way** is a laneway in Fremantle that runs for one block between
[Wood Street](./Wood_Street.html) and [Amherst Street](./Amherst_Street.html),
near the corner of Stirling Highway and Marmion Street.

It was named after the Adcock brothers.
Frank Henry Burton Adcock (d.1915) and Fredrick Brenchley Adcock (d.1915)
were soldiers in the 11th Battalion of the AIF who were killed at the Gallipoli landing in 1915.
They lived on [Solomon Street](Solomon_Street.html).
